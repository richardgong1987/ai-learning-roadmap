# Lesson 18 - Chain Rule

Main idea: When one thing depends on another thing, and that thing depends on something else, multiply the rates of change.

Formula:

dy/dx = (dy/du)(du/dx)

Example:

y=(3x+1)^2
u=3x+1

dy/du=2u

du/dx=3

Therefore:

dy/dx=2(3x+1)*3=6(3x+1)

Machine Learning:

Backpropagation in neural networks is an application of the chain rule.
