# Lesson Note: PTE Core Lesson 1

Date: 2026-06-23
Lesson: 1. Write Email: Task Format
Task Type: Write Email
Status: Completed

## Lesson Goal

Train the basic PTE Core Write Email format.

## Task Summary

Richard wrote an apology email to a friend after losing a borrowed book.

The email needed to include:

1. What happened.
2. An apology.
3. A solution.

## Performance Summary

Richard successfully included all required content points and used a recognizable email format.

Estimated performance: approximately 75–80%.

## Strengths

- Clear apology.
- Clear solution: buy a replacement copy.
- Appropriate email structure with greeting and closing.
- The message was understandable.

## Main Problems

- Some expressions were unnatural.
- Some grammar and preposition choices need improvement.
- Some punctuation was missing.

## Key Corrections

- Use "I am writing to apologize for..." in email openings.
- Use "on my bookshelf", not "in my bookshelf".
- Use "I have searched everywhere", not unnatural long expressions.
- Use "give you a replacement copy", not "return you a new one".

## Sentence Patterns To Remember

1. I am writing to apologize for...
2. Unfortunately, I have to admit that...
3. To make up for this, I will...
