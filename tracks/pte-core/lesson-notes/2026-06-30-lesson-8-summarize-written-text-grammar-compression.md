# Lesson Note - PTE Core Lesson 8

Date: 2026-06-30
Lesson: 8. Summarize Written Text: Grammar Compression
Skill: Writing
Task Type: Summarize Written Text

## Lesson Focus

The focus of this lesson was grammar compression: putting several ideas into one clear sentence without making the sentence too long or grammatically weak.

## Practice Topic

AI adoption in companies, including productivity, training, checking, communication, fairness, and human responsibility.

## Richard's Performance

Richard wrote a valid one-sentence summary and understood the basic contrast in the passage: AI improves productivity, but it also creates challenges.

## Strengths

- The answer was one sentence.
- The answer stayed within the word limit.
- The answer used a contrast structure with "but".
- The answer identified productivity and challenges as the two main sides of the passage.
- The answer included the idea of training, which was an important part of the passage.

## Main Issues

- "Introduce AI for improving productivity" should be "introduce AI to improve productivity".
- "It has also introduced many new challenges" is understandable but slightly unclear; "AI adoption also creates many challenges" is better.
- The answer focused mainly on training, but the passage also mentions careful checking, communication, fairness, and human responsibility.
- "Many new challenges" is too general; "practical and ethical challenges" is more precise.

## Corrected Version

The passage explains that many companies introduce AI to improve productivity, but AI adoption also creates practical and ethical challenges, so companies need proper training, careful checking, clear communication, and human responsibility rather than simply buying new tools.

## Teaching Point

When a passage gives several solutions, Richard should compress them into a short list or a wider noun phrase.

Bad compression:

Companies need training.

Better compression:

Companies need training, careful checking, clear communication, and human responsibility.

Even better when shorter is needed:

Companies need responsible AI management.
