# Lesson Note - PTE Core Lesson 9

Date: 2026-06-30
Lesson: 9. Writing Mixed Practice
Skill: Writing
Task Type: Write Email

## Lesson Focus

The focus of this lesson was mixed writing practice using a PTE Core Write Email task.

## Practice Topic

Requesting missing login details for an online English course that starts tomorrow morning.

## Richard's Performance

Richard completed all required task points clearly and used a polite tone. The email was easy to understand and stayed within the required word range.

## Strengths

- Explained the missing login details.
- Mentioned that the course starts tomorrow morning.
- Asked for the login details as soon as possible.
- Used a polite request.
- Used a clear email structure.

## Main Issues

- "I hop you are will" should be "I hope you are well".
- The closing lines need punctuation: "Thank you for your help." and "Best regards,".
- The sentence starting with "So" is understandable, but a more formal email can use "Therefore" or connect it to the previous sentence.

## Corrected Version

Dear Course Support Team,

I hope you are well. I registered for the online English course, but I have not yet received the login details. The course starts tomorrow morning, so I need the information urgently. Could you please send me the login details by email as soon as possible?

Thank you for your help.

Best regards,
Hanjin

## Teaching Point

For Write Email tasks, Richard should first check three things before submitting:

1. Did I answer all bullet points?
2. Is my tone polite?
3. Did I check common phrases and punctuation?
