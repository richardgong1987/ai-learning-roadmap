Date: 2026-06-29
Lesson: 7. Summarize Written Text: Main Idea
Skill: Writing
Task Type: Summarize Written Text

## Lesson Focus

The focus of this lesson was identifying the real main idea of a passage, not just listing details from the passage.

## Practice Topic

Reducing traffic congestion in cities through transport choices, public transport, street redesign, and technology.

## Richard's Performance

Richard wrote one sentence and stayed within the required word limit. This shows good control of the basic Summarize Written Text form.

## Strengths

- The answer was one sentence.
- The answer stayed within the word limit.
- The answer included several important details from the passage.
- The answer showed understanding that public transport, walking, cycling, and technology were relevant.

## Main Issues

- The answer listed "building more roads" as one of the solutions, but the passage says this is not a complete solution.
- The answer focused on details more than the writer's main opinion.
- The grammar pattern after "by" was not parallel.
- "Improve management efficiently" sounded unnatural.

## Corrected Version

Many cities are trying to reduce traffic congestion by combining better public transport, safer walking and cycling routes, and smarter traffic technology rather than simply building more roads.

## Teaching Point

For Summarize Written Text, Richard should first find the writer's opinion.

In this passage, the writer's opinion is:

Cities should not only try to move more cars; they should give people better travel choices.
