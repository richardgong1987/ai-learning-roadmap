# Lesson Note - PTE Core Lesson 6

Date: 2026-06-29
Lesson: 6. Summarize Written Text: One-Sentence Summary
Task Type: Summarize Written Text
Topic: Remote work and office work balance

## Lesson Summary

Richard practised a PTE Core Summarize Written Text task. The goal was to summarize the whole text in one clear sentence.

The text discussed remote work. It explained that working from home can save travel time and provide flexibility, but it can also slow communication and make employees feel isolated. The main idea was that many companies are trying to balance remote work and office work.

## Strengths

- The answer was one sentence.
- The answer included the main idea.
- Richard understood the contrast between benefits and problems.
- The answer stayed close to the required 20–35 word range.

## Main Problems

- Grammar accuracy needs improvement.
- “work at home” should be “working from home”.
- “introduce communication” is unnatural and should be replaced by “cause communication problems”.
- “feel isolated from team” should be “make employees feel isolated from their team”.
- The disadvantages should be expressed as clear noun phrases, such as “communication problems and isolation”.

## Key Teaching Point

For Summarize Written Text, Richard should write one clean sentence with this simple shape:

Main idea + because + benefit + but + problem.

Example:

Many companies are trying to balance remote work and office work because working from home offers flexibility, but it can also cause communication problems and isolation.

## Useful Patterns

1. Many companies are trying to balance A and B because...
2. Working from home offers + benefit, but it can also cause + problem.
3. Although A has benefits, it can also create problems such as B and C.
