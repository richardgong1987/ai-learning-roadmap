# Lesson Note: PTE Core Lesson 3

Date: 2026-06-28
Lesson: 3. Write Email: Opening and Closing
Task Type: Write Email
Status: Completed

## Lesson Goal

Practice clear and polite email openings and closings in a PTE Core Write Email task.

## Task Summary

Richard wrote an email to a manager requesting leave for a medical appointment.

The email needed to include:

1. Why he was writing.
2. When he would be away.
3. A polite request for approval.

## Performance Summary

Richard clearly explained the purpose and used a proper email format. The tone was polite and professional.

Estimated performance: approximately 80–85%.

## Strengths

- Clear purpose.
- Polite tone.
- Good email structure.
- Practical handover information included.

## Main Problems

- The phrase "one day off" conflicted with a two-day date range.
- Some expressions were unnatural.
- Business email phrases should be smoother.

## Key Corrections

- Use "I am writing to request one day of leave on...".
- Use "During my absence" or "While I am away".
- Use "Yang-san will take over my tasks".
- Use "I would appreciate it if you could approve my request".

## Sentence Patterns To Remember

1. I am writing to request one day of leave on...
2. During my absence, [Name] will take over my tasks.
3. I would appreciate it if you could approve my request.
