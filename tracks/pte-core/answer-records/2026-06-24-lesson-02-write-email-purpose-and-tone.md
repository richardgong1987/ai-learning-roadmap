## 7. Corrected Version

Dear Apartment Management Office,

I hope you are doing well.

I recently moved into Ojima-6 Apartment, but I found that the internet connection is not working properly.

I have tried restarting the router and checking all cable connections, but the problem still remains.

As a software engineer, I work from home full-time and rely heavily on a stable internet connection. This issue has a significant impact on my work and daily activities.

I would appreciate your assistance in resolving this issue as soon as possible.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,

Hanjin
