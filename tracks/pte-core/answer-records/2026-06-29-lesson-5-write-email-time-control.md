# PTE Core Writing Answer Record

Date: 2026-06-29
Task Type: Write Email
Lesson: 5. Write Email: Time Control
Topic: Urgent request for login details

## 1. Original Question

You registered for an online English course, but you have not received the login details. The course starts tomorrow morning.

Write an email to the course support team.

In your email, you should:

1. Explain the problem.
2. Say why it is urgent.
3. Ask them to send the login details as soon as possible.

## 2. Exam Instructions

Write a short and polite email.
Use simple, correct sentences.
Do not write an essay.
Do not use only bullet points.
Try to finish in 9 minutes.

## 3. Richard's Answer

Hi Julia,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to express that I unable to login my English course. Even through you have posted the login details on the Jira.

But I can’t login Jira either.

Since my English course will start tomorrow morning. It is quite urgent.

Could you please sent the login details to me through email directly as soon as possible?

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards

Hanjin

## 6. Mistake Analysis

### Content Problems

No serious content problem. The answer covers all three required points.

One small issue: the original task says the login details were not received. Richard added that the details were posted on Jira but he cannot access Jira. This is still logical, but it makes the email slightly more complicated than necessary.

### Form Problems

The email format is good, but there are too many paragraph breaks. In the real exam, a simpler structure is better.

### Grammar Problems

Bad: I unable to login my English course.
Good: I am unable to log in to my English course.

Bad: Even through you have posted the login details on the Jira.
Good: Even though you have posted the login details on Jira.

Bad: But I can’t login Jira either.
Good: I cannot log in to Jira either.

Bad: Since my English course will start tomorrow morning. It is quite urgent.
Good: Since my English course starts tomorrow morning, this is quite urgent.

Bad: Could you please sent the login details...
Good: Could you please send the login details...

Bad: through email
Good: by email

### Vocabulary Problems

“I am writing to express that” is too heavy and unnatural here.

Better: “I am writing to let you know that...”

“Thank you for your understanding” is acceptable, but “Thank you for your help” is more natural for a request.

## 7. Corrected Version

Hi Julia,

I hope you are well.

I am writing to let you know that I am unable to log in to my online English course. I understand that the login details may have been posted on Jira, but I cannot access Jira either.

Since my English course starts tomorrow morning, this is quite urgent. Could you please send the login details to me directly by email as soon as possible?

Thank you for your help.

Best regards,
Hanjin

## 8. Model Answer

Dear Course Support Team,

I hope you are well. I registered for the online English course, but I have not received the login details yet. The course starts tomorrow morning, so I need the information urgently.

Could you please send me the login details by email as soon as possible?

Thank you for your help.

Best regards,
Hanjin

## 9. Useful Sentence Patterns

1. I am writing to let you know that I am unable to + verb.
2. Since + situation, this is quite urgent.
3. Could you please send me + item + by email as soon as possible?

## 10. What Richard Should Improve Next

Focus on simple verb patterns under time pressure:

- be unable to do something
- log in to a system
- send something by email
- Since + sentence, sentence

Do not use over-complicated expressions when a simple sentence is enough.
