## 1. Original Question

Many people now work from home because modern technology allows them to communicate with colleagues, attend meetings, and complete tasks online. This can save travel time and give workers more flexibility in their daily lives. However, working from home can also make communication slower and may cause some employees to feel isolated from their team. For this reason, many companies are trying to find a balance between remote work and office work.

## 2. Exam Instructions

Try to write 20–35 words.

## 6. Mistake Analysis

### Content Problems

No serious content problem. Richard understood the main idea of the text.

One small improvement: the disadvantages should be expressed more clearly as “slower communication and feelings of isolation”.

### Form Problems

No serious form problem. The answer is one sentence and has a suitable length.

### Grammar Problems

Bad: work at home has many benefits
Good: working from home has many benefits

Bad: but also introduce communication
Good: but it can also cause communication problems

Bad: or feel isolated from team
Good: and make employees feel isolated from their team

Bad: from team
Good: from their team

### Vocabulary Problems

“work at home” is understandable, but “working from home” is more natural.

“introduce communication” is not natural. Use “cause communication problems” or “make communication slower”.

## 7. Corrected Version

Many companies are trying to balance remote work and office work because working from home offers flexibility and saves travel time, but it can also cause communication problems and isolation.

## 8. Model Answer

Many companies are seeking a balance between remote and office work because working from home offers flexibility and saves travel time, but it can also slow communication and make employees feel isolated.

## 9. Useful Sentence Patterns

1. Many companies are trying to balance A and B because...
2. Working from home offers + benefit, but it can also cause + problem.
3. Although A has benefits, it can also create problems such as B and C.

## 10. What Richard Should Improve Next

Focus on changing ideas into clean noun phrases:

- communication is slower → slower communication
- employees feel isolated → feelings of isolation
- work at home → working from home

Also remember that after “can”, use the base verb:

- can cause
- can make
- can slow
