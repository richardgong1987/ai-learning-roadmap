# PTE Core Writing Answer Record

Date: 2026-06-30
Task Type: Write Email
Lesson: 9. Writing Mixed Practice
Topic: Requesting login details for an online English course

## 1. Original Question

You recently booked an online English course, but you have not received the login information. The course starts tomorrow morning, and you are worried that you may miss the first class.

Write an email to the course support team.

In your email, you should:

- explain the problem
- mention when the course starts
- ask them to send the login details as soon as possible

## 2. Exam Instructions

Write 50-120 words. Use a polite and clear tone.

## 3. Richard's Answer

Dear course support team,

I hop you are will. I registered for the online English course, but I have not yet received the login details. The course starts tomorrow morning. 
So I need the information urgently. Could you send me the login details by email as soon as possible?

Thank you for your help

Best regards

Hanjin

## 4. Scoring Rubric

The answer should explain the missing login details, say the course starts tomorrow morning, and ask support to send the details quickly.

### Form

### Grammar

The email should use correct sentence structure, spelling, and punctuation.

### Vocabulary

The wording should be polite, clear, and suitable for a support-team email.

### Overall

A strong answer should complete all task points in clear, polite English with only minor language errors.

## 6. Mistake Analysis

### Content Problems

No major content problem. Richard included all required points: the problem, the start time, and the request.

### Form Problems

The email format is clear and the word count is within the required range.

### Grammar Problems

Bad: "I hop you are will."

Good: "I hope you are well."

Bad: "So I need the information urgently."

Better: "Therefore, I need the information urgently." or "I would appreciate it if you could send the information urgently."

### Vocabulary Problems

The vocabulary is mostly natural. "Login details" and "as soon as possible" are good phrases for this task.

## 7. Corrected Version

Dear Course Support Team,

I hope you are well. I registered for the online English course, but I have not yet received the login details. The course starts tomorrow morning, so I need the information urgently. Could you please send me the login details by email as soon as possible?

Thank you for your help.

Best regards,
Hanjin
