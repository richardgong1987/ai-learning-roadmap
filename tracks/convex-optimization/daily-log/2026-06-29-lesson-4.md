Completed lesson: Lesson 4 - Convex Sets

Summary:

Today the lesson covered the meaning of a convex set. The core intuition is that if two points are inside a set, the whole straight line segment between them must also stay inside the set.

Key formula:

For x and y in S, and theta between 0 and 1, the point theta x plus one minus theta times y must also be in S.

Applications discussed:

- Machine learning feasible parameter spaces
- Finance portfolio-weight constraints
- Engineering design constraints
- Optimization feasible regions
